## Workspace

A **workspace** is your team's shared environment. It contains:

- Topics you're monitoring
- All mentions and analytics
- Team members and billing
- Reports, workflows, and settings

Most customers use one workspace per organization. Agencies may use separate workspaces per client.

## Topics

A **topic** is a monitored subject — a brand, competitor, executive, product line, or narrative you want to track.

Each topic has:

- **Name** — how it appears in the UI
- **Keywords** — terms Grapevine matches against incoming content
- **Color** — for visual identification in charts and lists
- **Settings** — channel scope, alert rules, and refresh behavior

Topics are ongoing monitors. For one-off research, use **Search** instead.

## Mentions

A **mention** is a single piece of coverage that matched a topic's keywords. Mentions include:

- Article or post title and URL
- Full text (when publicly available)
- Published date and source
- Sentiment score
- Channel (news, X, Reddit, AI, etc.)
- Influence score — how much reach the story likely has

You can tag, comment on, archive, and assign mentions from the Inbox.

## Channels

**Channels** are the types of sources Grapevine monitors:

| Channel | Description | Plans |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **News & web** | Online news, blogs, forums | All plans |
| **Social** | X, Reddit, and other platforms | All plans |
| **Print** | Print publications | Plus and above |
| **Broadcast** | TV and radio | Pro and above |
| **AI** | LLM responses to tracked prompts | Plus and above |

Filter any view by channel using the global filter bar.

## Entities

**Entities** are people, organizations, and places extracted from your coverage. The Entities view shows who's being talked about across topics — useful for mapping spokespeople, competitors, and recurring journalists.

## Prompts (AI monitoring)

**Prompts** are questions Grapevine asks AI models on your behalf — e.g. "What is Acme Corp known for?" Grapevine tracks how answers change over time and which sources models cite. Available on Plus and Pro plans.

## Narratives & insights

Grapevine clusters related mentions into **narratives** — the stories driving your coverage. **Insights** surfaces trending narratives automatically with AI-generated briefs.

## Agent

**Agent** is Grapevine's built-in AI analyst. Ask it questions in natural language — "What spiked overnight?", "Summarize sentiment this week", "Draft a report on the pricing backlash" — and it queries your real workspace data.

## Searches vs topics

|  | **Topic** | **Search** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Purpose | Ongoing monitoring | One-off research |
| Alerts | Yes | No |
| Appears in dashboard | Yes | No |
| History | Retained per plan | Session-based |

Use topics for always-on listening. Use search when you need to investigate something quickly.
