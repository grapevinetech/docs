## Workspace

A **workspace** is your team's shared environment. It contains topics, mentions, analytics, members, billing, reports, workflows, and settings. Most customers use one workspace per organization; agencies may use separate workspaces per client.

<Frame caption="Workspace name, logo, timezone, and tags">

</Frame>

## Topics

A **topic** is an ongoing monitor — a brand, competitor, executive, product line, or narrative. You define it with the **topic builder**: keywords, optional themes, and Advanced settings for sources, history, and language. Grapevine collects mentions continuously and surfaces them on the dashboard, in analytics, and in alerts.

For one-off research, use **Search** instead.

## Mentions

A **mention** is a single piece of coverage that matched a topic. Each row includes title, source, sentiment, channel, date, and influence score. Open a mention for full text (when available), tags, comments, and assignments.

## Channels

**Channels** are the types of sources Grapevine monitors:

| Channel | Description | Plans |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **News & web** | Online news, blogs, forums | All plans |
| **Social** | X, Reddit, and other platforms | All plans |
| **Print** | Print publications | Plus and above |
| **Broadcast** | TV and radio | Pro and above |
| **AI** | LLM responses to tracked prompts | Plus and above |

Filter any view by channel using the global filter bar on Mentions, Dashboard, Analytics, and more.

## Entities

**Entities** are people, organizations, and places extracted from mentions and AI responses. Each entity gets a profile with mention volume, sentiment, activity, and prompt appearances.

## Prompts (AI monitoring)

**Prompts** are questions Grapevine asks AI models on your behalf — e.g. "What is [Brand] known for?" Results track visibility, sentiment, cited sources, and how answers change over time.

## Narratives & insights

Grapevine clusters related mentions into **narratives** — the stories driving your coverage. **Insights** surfaces trending narratives automatically with momentum scores and AI-generated briefs.

## Agent

**Agent** is Grapevine's built-in AI analyst. Ask questions in natural language — "What spiked overnight?", "Summarize sentiment this week", "Draft a holding statement" — and it queries your real workspace data.

## Searches vs topics

|  | **Topic** | **Search** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Purpose | Ongoing monitoring | One-off investigation |
| Alerts | Yes | No |
| Appears in dashboard | Yes | No |
| History | Retained per plan | Kept after capture; promote to a topic for ongoing monitoring |

Use topics for always-on listening. Use search when you need to investigate something quickly.
