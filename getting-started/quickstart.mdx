Get from signup to your first mentions in a few steps.

## 1. Create your account

Go to [app.grapevine.tech](https://app.grapevine.tech) and sign up with email or SSO.

You'll land in onboarding where you:

1. **Name your workspace** — your team's shared home for topics and mentions
2. **Choose a plan** — every plan includes a 7-day trial; add a card to start
3. **Complete checkout** — billing is handled securely via Stripe

<Note>
  If you're joining an existing team, accept the invite email instead of creating a new workspace.
</Note>

<Frame caption="Workspace settings after signup">
  <img src="https://static.grapevine.tech/docs/screenshots/app-settings.png?v=10" alt="Grapevine workspace settings" />
</Frame>

## 2. Add your first topic

Topics are what Grapevine listens for. Open **Topics** and use the **"What do you want to monitor?"** builder:

1. Add **keywords** — brand name, products, executives, `@` handles (comma-separated)
2. Optionally add **themes** — plain-language subjects Grapevine expands across channels
3. Optionally set a **name** and open **Advanced** for sources, history, and language
4. Click **Create topic**

Grapevine begins ingesting within minutes. Volume depends on how broad your keywords are.

<Frame caption="The topic builder and your active monitors">
  <img src="https://static.grapevine.tech/docs/screenshots/app-topics.png?v=11" alt="Grapevine topics" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Start narrow. A focused topic with 3–5 precise keywords produces cleaner results than one bloated list.
</Tip>

## 3. Browse your mentions

Open **Mentions** to see incoming coverage across all topics. Each mention includes:

- **Title and source** — where the story was published
- **Full text** — when publicly available, not just a snippet
- **Sentiment** — AI-scored tone of the coverage
- **Channel** — news, social, AI, broadcast, etc.

Use the filter bar to narrow by topic, date range, channel, or tag.

  <img src="https://static.grapevine.tech/docs/screenshots/app-mentions.png?v=10" alt="Grapevine mentions" />

## 4. Explore the dashboard

The **Dashboard** is your morning briefing:

- Total mentions and reach over your selected period
- Sentiment breakdown
- Top stories ranked by influence
- Share of voice across topics
- Spike alerts when volume jumps unexpectedly

<Frame caption="Mentions, sentiment, and top stories at a glance">
  <img src="https://static.grapevine.tech/docs/screenshots/app-dashboard.png?v=10" alt="Grapevine dashboard" />
</Frame>

## 5. Configure notifications

Go to **Settings → Notifications** to control:

- **Email digest** — periodic summary of mentions and narratives
- **Workflow alerts** — emails when automated rules fire
- **Comment mentions** — when a teammate @-mentions you
