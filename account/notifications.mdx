Manage what emails Grapevine sends you under **Settings → Notifications**.

## Monitoring

| Notification | Default | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Email digest** | On | Periodic brief summarizing mentions, sentiment, and narratives |
| **Workflow alerts** | On | Email when one of your workflows triggers |

## Collaboration

| Notification | Default | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Comment mentions** | On | Email when a teammate @-mentions you in a comment |

## Product

| Notification | Default | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **News & updates** | On | Product announcements, new features, and tips from the Grapevine team |

## Podcast briefings

<Info>
  Podcast briefings are available on **Plus** and **Pro** plans.
</Info>

Your morning coverage compiled as a daily audio digest — ready for your commute or hands-free at your desk. Enable in Settings alongside your notification preferences.

## Audio calls

For urgent moments, **audio calls** route a spoken intelligence update directly to your phone. Available on Plus and Pro.

<Tip>
  Turn off "News & updates" if you only want operational alerts. Keep "Email digest" on unless you live in the Inbox all day.
</Tip>
