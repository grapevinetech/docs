## Free trial

Every plan includes a **7-day free trial**. You'll add a payment method at signup but won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime during the trial with no charge.

## Managing billing

From **Settings → Billing** you can:

- View your current plan and renewal date
- Open the **Stripe billing portal** to update payment method, download invoices, or cancel
- Upgrade or downgrade plans

## Upgrading

Upgrades take effect immediately. You're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing period.

## Downgrading

Downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period. If you exceed the new plan's limits (topics, users), you'll need to reduce usage before the downgrade takes effect.
