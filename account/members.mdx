Grapevine workspaces are built for teams. Manage who has access under **Settings → Members**.

## Inviting members

1. Go to **Settings → Members**
2. Enter an email address
3. Click **Invite**

Invitees receive an email with a link to join your workspace. They'll share your topics, mentions, and billing plan.

## Member limits by plan

| Plan | Users included |
| --- | --- |
| Starter | 3 |
| Plus | 10 |
| Pro | Unlimited |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

## Removing members

Workspace admins can remove members from the Members tab. Removed users lose access immediately but their comments and activity history remain.

## Roles

All members in a workspace can view and collaborate on mentions. Billing and workspace settings are restricted to the workspace owner.
