**Analytics** is Grapevine's deep-dive view for trends over time. Use it when you need charts for a report, board deck, or quarterly review.

</Frame>

## Available charts

- **Mention volume over time** — daily/weekly trends
- **Sentiment trend** — how tone shifts across a period
- **Share of voice** — relative mention share across topics
- **Channel breakdown** — news vs social vs AI vs broadcast
- **Top sources** — outlets ranked by mention count and influence
- **Entity mentions** — people and organizations appearing most often

## Filtering and comparison

Use the global filter bar to scope analytics to specific topics, date ranges, channels, and tags. Compare periods by adjusting the date range.

## Exporting data

Analytics views can be included in **Reports** (see Collaboration → Reports). For programmatic access, Pro plans include **API access**.

## History retention

How far back you can chart depends on your plan:

| Plan | History |
| --- | --- |

## Performance note

Headline KPIs on the Dashboard are computed server-side for speed. Analytics charts render from your filtered mention set — large date ranges with many topics may take a moment to load.
