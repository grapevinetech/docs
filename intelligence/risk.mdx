**Risk intelligence** flags emerging threats to your reputation before they become crises — regulatory scrutiny, activist pressure, supply-chain exposure, narrative shifts in the press.

<Info>
  Risk intelligence is included on **Plus**, **Pro**, and **Enterprise** plans.
</Info>

## What Grapevine watches for

- **Volume spikes** — sudden increases in negative mentions
- **Narrative shifts** — new story angles gaining traction
- **Source escalation** — tier-1 outlets picking up a story that started on social
- **Cross-channel spread** — a story jumping from Reddit to news to AI responses
- **Entity risk signals** — key people or organizations appearing in negative context

## Risk view

Open **Risk** from the sidebar to see a prioritized feed of risk signals across your workspace. Each signal includes:

- Severity indicator
- Affected topic(s)
- Summary of the threat
- Related mentions
- Recommended actions

## Combining with other features

| Tool | Role in risk management |
| --- | --- |
| **Dashboard spike alerts** | First signal that something is moving |
| **Insights** | Understand the narrative driving the risk |
| **Workflows** | Automate alerts to the right people |
| **Audio calls** | Urgent spoken briefings for critical moments (Plus\+) |
| **Agent** | Ask "What's the biggest risk right now?" for instant analysis |

## Audio calls

On Plus and Pro, **audio calls** deliver a spoken intelligence update directly to your phone — for moments that can't wait for a screen. Configure in Settings.

<Warning>
  Risk intelligence surfaces signals early — but human judgment decides what's a real crisis. Always verify before responding publicly.
</Warning>
