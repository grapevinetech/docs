People increasingly ask AI before they search, shop, or decide. **AI monitoring** tracks what leading models say about your brand — and how those answers change.

## How it works

1. You create **prompts** — questions like "What is Acme Corp?" or "Is Acme Corp a good employer?"
2. Grapevine sends these prompts to AI models on a schedule
3. Responses are stored, scored, and tracked over time
4. You see visibility trends, cited sources, and sentiment shifts

## Supported models

| Plan | Models monitored |
| --- | --- |
| **Plus** | ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews |
| **Pro** | All Plus models, plus Claude, Gemini, Grok, and DeepSeek |
| **Enterprise** | 10\+ LLMs, custom configuration |

## Creating prompts

Go to **Prompts** in the sidebar:

1. Click **Add prompt**
2. Write a natural-language question a consumer might ask
3. Assign it to a topic
4. Choose which models to query (based on your plan)

<Tip>
  Write prompts the way real users ask — "Is \[Brand\] trustworthy?" beats "Brand reputation analysis".
</Tip>

## Reading results

Each prompt shows:

- **Latest response** from each model
- **Visibility score** — how prominently your brand appears
- **Cited sources** — URLs the model references
- **History** — how answers evolved over weeks and months

## Why it matters

A bad AI answer can spread faster than a bad headline. AI monitoring lets you catch inaccurate or negative model responses before they become the default answer millions of people see.
