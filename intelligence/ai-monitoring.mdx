## Why AI monitoring matters

Traditional media monitoring misses a growing channel: **AI assistants**. When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity about your brand, the answer shapes perception — often without you knowing it was asked.

Grapevine tracks these answers over time so you can:

- Catch **inaccurate** or **negative** model responses early
- See which **sources** models cite when describing you
- Measure **visibility** trends as models update their training and retrieval

## How it fits together

1. Create **prompts** — questions real users might ask
2. Grapevine queries **AI models** on a schedule
3. Responses are **scored and stored** with cited sources
4. **Entities** and **sources** from AI answers merge into your workspace directory

<Tip>
  Start with 3–5 prompts that mirror how customers, journalists, or investors actually ask about you.
</Tip>
