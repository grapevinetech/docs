The **Dashboard** is the first screen most teams open each day. It summarizes what's happening across your topics.

## KPI tiles

At a glance you'll see:

- **Mentions** — total count for the selected period
- **Reach** — estimated audience exposure
- **Sentiment** — aggregate tone (positive / neutral / negative split)
- **Share of voice** — how your topics compare to each other

KPIs respect your global filters (topic, date range, channel, tag).

## Top stories

A carousel of the highest-influence mentions, ranked by reach and recency. These are the stories worth reading first — not just the newest.

## Narrative highlights

AI-generated summaries of the dominant narratives in your coverage. Each highlight links to related mentions so you can drill in.

## AI visibility

<Info>
  Available on **Plus** and **Pro** plans.
</Info>

Shows how visible your brand is across tracked AI prompts — a gauge of how often models mention you and with what sentiment.

## Spike alerts

When mention volume jumps significantly above baseline, Grapevine surfaces an **anomaly alert** at the top of the Dashboard. These early warnings help you catch emerging crises before they peak.

## Topic breakdown

A per-topic summary showing mention counts and sentiment for each monitor in scope. Quickly see which brand or competitor is driving activity.

## Recent mentions

The latest five mentions for quick scanning, with links to the full Inbox.

## Empty state

If you haven't added topics yet, the Dashboard prompts you to create your first topic. Without topics, there's nothing to chart.
