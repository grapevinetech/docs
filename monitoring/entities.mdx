The **Entities** view shows who and what is being talked about across your topics — spokespeople, competitors, institutions, and locations that recur in your coverage.

## Entity types

Grapevine extracts three kinds of entities from mentions and AI responses:

| Type | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| **People** | Executives, journalists, elected officials |
| **Organizations** | Companies, agencies, parties, NGOs |
| **Locations** | Cities, regions, landmarks |

Each entity gets a profile with mention volume, sentiment, and recent coverage.

## Why entities matter

- Map the **cast of characters** around your topics
- Spot when a new journalist or critic enters the conversation
- Compare how different **organizations** are framed in the same narrative
- Track **geographic** angles in regional or national stories

## AI entity appearances

On Plus and Pro plans, entities mentioned in **AI model responses** are merged into the same directory as mention-derived entities — so you see the full picture of who models associate with your topics.

## Filtering entities

Use the global filter bar to scope entities by topic, date, channel, and tag. Filter by type (people, organizations, locations) to focus your analysis.
