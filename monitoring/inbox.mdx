title: "Inbox"
description: "Notifications when teammates tag you in coverage discussions."
---

Your **Inbox** is for team collaboration alerts — not your coverage feed. When a teammate @-mentions you in a comment on a mention, it appears here.

## What shows up

- **Comment mentions** — a teammate tagged you in a thread
- **Assignments** — coverage routed to you for follow-up
- **Workflow notifications** — actions triggered by your automation rules

## Mentions vs Inbox

| | **Mentions** | **Inbox** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Purpose | Browse incoming coverage | Team notifications |
| Content | News, social, AI matches | Comments and assignments |
| Filters | Topic, date, channel, tag | Unread / read status |

For triaging coverage, use **[Mentions](/monitoring/mentions)**. Use Inbox to stay on top of what your team needs from you.

## Notifications

Configure email alerts for Inbox activity under **[Settings → Notifications](/account/notifications)**.
