The **Prompts** page is where you manage the questions Grapevine sends to AI models on your behalf — and review how answers change over time.

<Info>
  AI monitoring is available on **Plus** and **Pro** plans.
</Info>

## Creating prompts

1. Click **New prompt**
2. Write a natural-language question a consumer might ask about your topic
3. Assign it to a topic
4. Choose which models to query (based on your plan)

<Tip>
  Write prompts the way real users ask — "Is \[Brand\] trustworthy?" beats "Brand reputation analysis".
</Tip>

## Reading results

Each prompt shows:

- **Latest response** from each model
- **Visibility score** — how prominently your brand appears
- **Cited sources** — URLs the model references
- **History** — how answers evolved over weeks and months

## Supported models

| Plan | Models monitored |
| --- | --- |
| **Plus** | ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews |
| **Pro** | All Plus models, plus Claude, Gemini, Grok, and DeepSeek |
| **Enterprise** | 10+ LLMs, custom configuration |

## Prompt limits

| Plan | Prompts per month |
| --- | --- |
| Plus | 50 |
| Pro | 200 |
| Enterprise | Custom |

## Why it matters

A bad AI answer can spread faster than a bad headline. Prompts let you catch inaccurate or negative model responses before they become the default answer millions of people see.
