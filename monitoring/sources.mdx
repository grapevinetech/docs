The **Sources** view maps the publications, platforms, and authors driving your coverage.

## Source profiles

Each source gets a profile showing:

- **Mention count** over your selected period
- **Average sentiment** of coverage from that outlet
- **Reach estimate** — audience size proxy
- **Recent mentions** — latest stories linking back to your topics

Click any source to open its full profile page.

## Why sources matter

Understanding _who_ is writing about you is as important as _what_ they're saying:

- Identify your most influential journalists and outlets
- Spot new platforms picking up your narrative
- Compare share of voice by publication tier
- Feed relationship-building for PR outreach

## Influence scoring

Grapevine scores each mention's likely influence based on source authority, engagement signals, and syndication reach. High-influence stories surface first on the Dashboard and in Agent responses.

## AI source citations

On Plus and Pro plans, AI monitoring tracks which sources LLMs cite when answering prompts about your brand. This shows up in **Prompts** and the Dashboard's AI visibility card — helping you understand which content shapes model answers.

## Filtering sources

Apply the same global filters (topic, date, channel, tag) to narrow the sources list. Useful for answering questions like "Which tech blogs covered us this quarter?" or "Which Reddit communities mention our competitor most?"
