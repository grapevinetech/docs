## Mention layout

Each mention row shows:

- **Title** and excerpt
- **Source** — publication or platform
- **Topic** — which monitor matched
- **Sentiment** — positive, neutral, or negative
- **Date** — when it was published
- **Channel** — news, social, AI, etc.

Click a mention to open the full detail view with complete text, metadata, and collaboration tools.

## Filtering

Use the global filter bar (available on Mentions, Dashboard, Analytics, and more):

- **Topics** — one or many
- **Date range** — today, this week, custom
- **Channels** — news, X, Reddit, AI, broadcast
- **Tags** — workspace-defined labels

Filters persist as you navigate between pages.

## Working with mentions

| Action | How |
| --- | --- |
| **Tag** | Add workspace tags for categorization ("crisis", "product", "competitor") |
| **Comment** | @-mention teammates to discuss a story |
| **Archive** | Remove from active views without deleting |
| **Assign** | Route to a team member for follow-up |

## Full article text

Where content is publicly available, Grapevine surfaces the **full article** — not just a headline and link. For paywalled publications, connect your existing news subscriptions in Settings to pull in that coverage automatically.

## Syndication handling

Grapevine deduplicates syndicated content. When the same AP wire story appears on 40 outlets, you see one primary mention with syndication context — not 40 identical alerts.

## Alerts

Configure email alerts per topic or workspace in **Settings → Notifications**. Starter plans include email alerts; higher tiers add workflow-based alerting and audio calls for urgent spikes.
