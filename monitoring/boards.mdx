**Boards** let you watch the accounts that matter most — reporters, outlets, spokespeople — in live-updating columns, without leaving Grapevine.

## How boards work

1. Create a board (e.g. "Capitol watch")
2. Add columns — each column follows a list, account, or search
3. Posts stream in newest-first, like a social command center

## Use cases

- Monitor **beat reporters** during a legislative session
- Track **competitor executives** on X
- Watch **official accounts** during a product launch or crisis
- Separate **outlets** from **individual voices** in different columns

## Working with posts

Each post card shows the author, text, media, and engagement. Open a post to see full context or save it to a mention thread for team discussion.
