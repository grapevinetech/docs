Grapevine is media monitoring built for the AI era. It pulls coverage from news, social, podcasts, broadcast, and AI assistants into one workspace — scored, clustered, and ready to act on.

<Info>
  Grapevine is currently in **early access**. Features and limits may evolve as we ship.
</Info>

## What Grapevine monitors

- **Online news & blogs** — articles, op-eds, and trade press across the open web
- **Social platforms** — conversations on X, Reddit, and other major networks
- **Print & broadcast** — print coverage and broadcast mentions (plan-dependent)
- **AI assistants** — how ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and other models describe your brand

Unlike alert tools that email you links, Grapevine **reads the coverage**: scoring sentiment, deduplicating syndicated stories, and surfacing the narratives that actually matter.

| Persona | How teams use Grapevine |

## Core workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a workspace">
    Sign up at [app.grapevine.tech](https://app.grapevine.tech), name your workspace, and start a free trial.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add topics">
    Define the brands, people, competitors, and themes you want Grapevine to listen for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your inbox">
    New mentions arrive continuously. Triage them like email — tag, comment, and assign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act on intelligence">
    Use the dashboard, insights, analytics, and Agent to understand narratives and respond fast.
  </Step>
</Steps>
