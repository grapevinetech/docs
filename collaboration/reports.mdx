## Creating a report

1. Go to **Reports** in the sidebar
2. Click **New report**
3. Choose a template or start blank
4. Add sections — mention summaries, charts, narrative highlights, sentiment breakdowns
5. Scope to topics and date ranges
6. Share via link or export

## Report sections

Common sections teams include:

- **Executive summary** — AI-generated overview of the period
- **Top mentions** — highest-influence stories
- **Sentiment analysis** — tone breakdown with trend
- **Share of voice** — competitive comparison
- **Narrative highlights** — key stories driving coverage
- **Recommendations** — suggested actions

## White-label reports

<Info>
  White-label reports (custom branding) are available on **Pro** and **Enterprise** plans.
</Info>

Remove Grapevine branding and add your agency or company logo for client deliverables.

## Scheduled delivery

Set reports to regenerate on a schedule — weekly for your team, monthly for leadership. Recipients get an email when a new version is ready.

## Agent-assisted reports

Ask **Agent** to "Generate a report on last week's pricing coverage" and it will pull real data from your workspace into a draft report you can refine.
