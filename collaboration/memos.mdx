**Memos** are collaborative documents inside Grapevine — holding statements, crisis plans, Q&A prep, and executive briefs written with your team (and Agent) in one place.

## When to use memos

- Draft a **holding statement** while monitoring breaks
- Build a **Q&A** for spokesperson media training
- Compile a **crisis comms plan** with assigned next steps
- Co-write with **Agent** — tag it in a mention thread and it drafts into a memo

## Collaboration

Memos support rich text, comments, and @-mentions — the same collaboration primitives as mention threads. Changes save automatically.

## Agent-assisted drafting

Ask **Agent** to draft a response to coverage and attach the output as a memo. Edit tone and facts before sharing with leadership or publishing.

See **[Agent](/collaboration/agent)** for more on AI-assisted workflows.
