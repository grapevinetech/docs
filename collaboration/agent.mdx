**Agent** is Grapevine's built-in AI analyst. Ask questions in natural language and it queries your real workspace data — never inventing mentions or stats.

## What Agent can do

### Read & analyze

- "Summarize mentions from the last 24 hours"
- "What's our sentiment trend this week?"
- "Show me the top stories about our competitor"
- "What spiked overnight?"
- "Compare share of voice across our three brands"

### Take action

When you ask clearly, Agent can also modify data:

- Tag or archive mentions
- Add comments
- Create memos and reports
- Update workflow configurations

<Warning>
  Agent can make bulk changes. For updates affecting more than 10 mentions, it will confirm before executing.
</Warning>

## How to use Agent

Open **Agent** from the sidebar and type your question. Agent selects the right tools automatically — searching mentions, pulling analytics, checking spike alerts, or generating reports.

## Tips for better answers

- **Be specific about time** — "today", "this week", "since Monday"
- **Name topics when you have many** — "for the Acme topic" narrows scope
- **Ask for summaries first** — "Summarize coverage" before "Write a report"

## Agent vs Insights

|  | **Agent** | **Insights** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Interaction | Conversational, on-demand | Automatic feed |
| Best for | Ad-hoc questions, actions | Passive narrative discovery |
| Data access | Full workspace tools | Narrative clustering |

## Mention cards

When Agent shows specific mentions, it renders interactive mention cards you can click to open the full detail view — not just plain-text links.
